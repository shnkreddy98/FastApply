Job Description: Empowering Server Options Product Management Join our dynamic team to shape the future of data-driven decision-making! In this high-impact role, you’ll develop insightful visualizations, conduct deep analytics, and drive strategic projects that support 20+ product managers overseeing a $7B server component business. Your work will uncover trends, optimize portfolio decisions, and enhance the way we manage a complex ecosystem of product options. If you’re passionate about transforming data into actionable intelligence and influencing a multi-billion-dollar business, this is the opportunity for you! Responsibilities: Lead the collection, organization, and management of data to power tools that support product management decision-making. Develop and maintain master data sets, ensuring accuracy, integrity, and usability for analytics. Enhance and optimize existing datasets to improve insights and drive strategic decision-making for the product management team. Support key initiatives such as Mainstream product offerings, forecasting processes, and end-of-life (EOL) planning. Collaborate cross-functionally to build data-driven solutions that improve operational efficiency and portfolio management. Identify opportunities for process improvement, automation and optimization through data analysis and visualization. Knowledge &amp; Skills: Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Data Analytics, or a related field (MBA or advanced technical degree preferred). 4+ years of experience in data analytics, visualization, or a related role, preferably in the technology or product management space. Strong technical understanding of data structures, industry trends, and server component ecosystems. Proven ability to translate complex data into actionable insights that empower decision-makers. Expertise in financial performance metrics, cost-efficient solution building, and strategic data analysis. Advanced proficiency in Excel, including macros and automation, with strong skills in Access. Experience with programming and automation tools (e.g., Python, SQL, Power BI, Tableau) to streamline data processes. Ability to work independently and collaboratively in a fast-paced, high-impact environment. Join us and make your mark!
